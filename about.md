#### Welcome to my page! 

My research interests lie in the boundary between market design and industrial organisation, combining theory and data. My current research mainly concerns how information affects the allocation of students to schools. 

Since September 2016, I am a Browaldh research fellow at [University of Gothenburg](http://www.gu.se/english){:target="_blank"}. I obtained my PhD from [ECARES](http://ecares.org){:target="_blank"}, [Université libre de Bruxelles](http://www.ulb.ac.be/){:target="_blank"} in August 2016, under the supervision of [Estelle Cantillon](http://ecantill.ulb.be/){:target="_blank"} and [Patrick Legros](http://plegros.net/){:target="_blank"}. Please find my [CV](https://lichen999.github.io/CV_lichen.pdf){:target="_blank"} here.
