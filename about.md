#### Welcome to my page! 

My research interests lie in the boundary between market design and industrial organisation, combining theory and data. My current [research](https://lichen999.github.io/research/) mainly concerns the design of matching market. 

Since September 2016, I am a Browaldh research fellow at [University of Gothenburg](http://www.gu.se/english){:target="_blank"}. I obtained my PhD from [ECARES](http://ecares.org){:target="_blank"}, [Université libre de Bruxelles](http://www.ulb.ac.be/){:target="_blank"} in August 2016, under the supervision of [Estelle Cantillon](http://ecantill.ulb.be/){:target="_blank"} and [Patrick Legros](http://plegros.net/){:target="_blank"}.
